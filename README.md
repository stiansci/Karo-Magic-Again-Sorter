**Take control of your leeches and prioritize your study by automatically distributing cards based on their failure rate!**

Are you several weeks into your Anki reviews and starting to struggle with the same difficult cards over and over? As exam day draws near, studying your whole deck isn't efficient anymore. You need a 'binning' strategy to separate the easy stuff and focus strictly on your hardest cards.

Karo Magic Lapse Sorter is a powerful, statistically robust tool that takes the guesswork out of your revision: it identifies your exact weak spots and automatically organizes them so you can tackle your leeches right when it matters most.

### What it does
This add-on analyzes a selected Superdeck and looks at the exact number of **lapses** (how many times you've forgotten and hit "Again" on a card). It then automatically sorts and physically moves all the cards into **4 new Subdecks (Buckets)**, ordered from your absolute worst cards to your best ones.

### How the "Magic" works (Statistically Balanced)
Instead of using arbitrary numbers (like "cards with 3-5 lapses"), this add-on uses **Rank-Based Quartile Binning**. It ranks all the cards in your deck from highest lapses to lowest, and splits them into 4 perfectly equal chunks:
*   **Bucket 1 (High Lapses):** The top 25% of cards you struggle with the most. Start your study session here to tackle your biggest weaknesses while your mind is fresh!
*   **Bucket 2 (Medium-High):** The next 25% of challenging cards.
*   **Bucket 3 (Medium-Low):** The 25% of cards you occasionally forget.
*   **Bucket 4 (Low/Zero):** The bottom 25% of cards you know very well (or new cards with 0 lapses).

### Features
*   **One-Click Organization:** Just select your Superdeck and click a button. The add-on creates the subdecks and moves the cards automatically.
*   **Deck Preset Inheritance:** The newly created Bucket subdecks automatically inherit the Deck Options/Preset of your original Superdeck, so your scheduling settings remain perfectly intact.
*   **Auto-Language Detection:** The UI automatically adapts to your Anki language! Currently supports **English**, **Italian**, and **Spanish**. 

### How to use
1. Open Anki and go to the top menu bar: **Tools** > **Karo Magic Lapse Sorter...**
2. A dialog will appear. Select the Superdeck you want to sort from the dropdown menu.
3. Click **"Sort and Distribute Cards"**.
4. Enjoy your newly organized deck and start destroying those leeches!
